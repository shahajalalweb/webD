Our School

our school goes by the name of Ghior D.N pilot high school. It is one of the best and oldest schools in the district of Manikgonj. Our school is a two-storied building. It is 500 feet long and 20 feet wide. It has a six fief wide verandah. There are 50 rooms. There are fifteen rooms all in our school. They are headmaster’s room, a teacher’s common room, an office room, a students’ common room, a prayer room, a well-furnished library room, and a well-equipped science laboratory. The other rooms are used for classes. There are about 1300 students and 50 teachers in our school. All the teachers are good and qualified. We have a big field in front of our school. The results of our school have all along been satisfactory. We hold annual drama and annual sports every year. We are very proud of our school.
